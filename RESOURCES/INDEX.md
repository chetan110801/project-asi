> How we *choose* resources (leverage, signal density, etc.): [`../INSTRUCTIONS/RESOURCE_COLLECTION_SYSTEM.md`](../INSTRUCTIONS/RESOURCE_COLLECTION_SYSTEM.md). This file is the *inventory*.

Those go to [`REQUESTS.md`](REQUESTS.md) for you to obtain legally. Exact links/editions below should be **verified** before relying on them (I avoid inventing URLs — that's the hallucination guard from QC §4).

## How an entry grows
1. Added here when chosen (with `id`, access, why it earned a slot).
2. `processing` → I read/extract (per [`../INSTRUCTIONS/KNOWLEDGE_PROCESSING_SYSTEM.md`](../INSTRUCTIONS/KNOWLEDGE_PROCESSING_SYSTEM.md)).
3. `processed` → its insights become a **learning module**; link the module's `id` here.
4. 🔴 items I can't access → also listed in [`REQUESTS.md`](REQUESTS.md) for you.

*Catalog is intentionally small. We add resources when a question or module needs them — not to build a giant unread pile (that anti-goal is in RESOURCE_COLLECTION_SYSTEM §1).*
